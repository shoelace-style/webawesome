Imported livestock declaration

Use this form to declare livestock you are bringing into the state. State law requires that this declaration be filed within 72 hours of the animals crossing state lines. Filings submitted after the deadline may be subject to penalties.

Knowingly making a false declaration on this form is a federal offense. Under 18 U.S.C. § 1001, false statements on this declaration are punishable by fines and imprisonment of up to five years.

What happens next

  1. The Department of Agriculture reviews your declaration. Review typically takes 1 to 2 business days.
  2. A field officer may contact you by phone if additional information is required.
  3. You will receive an approval notice by email at the address provided in this declaration.
