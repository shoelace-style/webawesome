Glasshouse — the digital team

The team behind the catalog

Glasshouse is the small in-house digital team responsible for the museum's online collection — roughly 280,000 specimens, photographs, and field notes drawn from more than a century of research. The team was established in 2021 to bring a public-facing record of the collection online, and to keep it accurate, accessible, and useful long after the initial launch. The decision to build the catalog in-house rather than contract it out was deliberate: a collection of this size needs people who understand the specimens, the cataloguing conventions, and the audiences who depend on the record. Six of us now do that work, supported by curators and conservators across the museum.
